Let's Start With Some Good Thoughts

Good thoughts are good to read. They are even better to write. Think of some good thoughts of your own. They don't have to feel true, just create some good thoughts, even if you can't believe in them yet.

Below are some good thoughts I created.

Good thoughts and good actions lead to good feelings, eventually. Crawl Out of Hell with me!

* I have exactly the right amount of money *

* I have the exact right job *

* I found the ideal man *

* I found the ideal exercise routine *

* I have many friends who care about me and who I care about *

* I have really good health *

* I am on the exact right path *

* I am completely safe *

* I have lots of safe fun *

* I am a trustworthy person with trustworthy friends *

* There is a purpose to life *
